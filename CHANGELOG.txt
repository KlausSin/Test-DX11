//////////////// added RenderFrameContext , remove crc from resource ////////////////

1. Eliminates global dependencies
 All rendering systems (terrain, skybox, characters, effects) 
 get the same snapshot of view/projection, camera, fog, shadow, and time. 
 No more scattered ms_matView, CCameraManager, or mc_pEnvironmentData.
 
2. Deterministic and frame-consistent rendering 
 Delta time, frame time, fog, and shadow are fixed per frame,
 making animations, water, wind, and effects consistent across all systems.
